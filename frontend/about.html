My Bold Journey ✨

I’m Purva Chalindrawar, a full-stack developer with a passion for creating impactful digital solutions. My journey spans both tech and non-tech fields, blending technical expertise with real-world problem-solving. Let’s explore the milestones that shaped my career!

🔄 A Leap of Faith

I started in Mechanical Engineering, designing machines, but my passion lay elsewhere. In 2022, I transitioned to Computer Science—a bold move that meant starting anew. Leaving behind years of study was daunting, but my drive to innovate in the tech world fueled my journey.

💡 Facing Barriers & Rising Strong

The shift to tech wasn’t seamless. I grappled with self-doubt and complex concepts like algorithms, often feeling behind my peers. Through late-night coding sessions, study groups, and countless tutorials, I persevered. Each small victory—like solving my first coding challenge—boosted my confidence, teaching me resilience and the power of persistence.

🚀 My First Tech Spark

Months into my tech journey, I joined a hackathon—and my team won! Our app addressed a real-world issue, and the victory affirmed my place in tech. It wasn’t just a trophy; it was a spark that ignited my passion for building innovative solutions in the tech field.

💼 Non-Tech Internship Adventures

An unexpected opportunity arose—an internship at a non-tech firm. They needed tech solutions, and I stepped up, automating their processes with custom tools. Bridging the gap between tech and non-tech teams was challenging, but I simplified communication and focused on their needs, building a system that saved hours weekly. This experience honed my adaptability and cross-domain collaboration skills.

🛠️ My Professional Tech Work

As a Full Stack Developer Intern at Indian Cyber Security Solutions, I worked on developing and deploying multiple dynamic web applications using the MERN stack. I focused on improving system performance, building clean and animated user interfaces, and designing scalable REST APIs. Collaborating in Agile sprints and using Git for version control, I also enhanced team communication through clear documentation and regular sync-ups. This experience significantly strengthened my skills in backend development, frontend design, and professional teamwork.

🌟 My Skills

I’m a full-stack developer with hands-on experience in JavaScript, React, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, Python, and AWS. My skillset also includes building RESTful APIs, designing microservices architectures, working with Docker containers, and managing Linux-based systems. With a solid foundation in computer networking and database management, I bring a strong backend and infrastructure focus. I’m currently expanding my expertise in cybersecurity concepts such as ethical hacking, while continuously strengthening my problem-solving and collaboration abilities through projects, internships, and hackathons.

🏅 Achievements That Shine

My journey is marked by milestones I’m proud of. I’m an AWS-certified Database Specialist and a Beta Microsoft Learn Student Ambassador. I’ve won hackathons, delivered impactful projects, and earned recognition for my contributions, reflecting my growth in the tech field.

🔍 What’s Next?

I’m now diving deeper into AI, cybersecurity, and system design, creating projects that merge tech with real-world impact—like AI models for data analysis and secure business systems. My goal is to continue growing, innovating, and making a difference in the tech field, one line of code at a time.

This is just the beginning—I’m shaping my future in tech, and I’m excited for what’s next! 💖
